# Company Policies & Terms of Service

This document is the reference the sales agent uses to answer questions about
policies, billing, and terms. Keep answers consistent with what is written here.

## About Us

We are a SaaS CRM company. Our product helps small and mid-sized sales teams
manage leads, automate follow-ups, and track deals. Plans range from a Starter
tier for very small teams up to an Enterprise tier with SSO and a dedicated
success manager.

## Free Trial

- Every new account gets a **14-day free trial** of the Pro plan.
- No credit card is required to start the trial.
- At the end of the trial, accounts move to the Starter plan unless an upgrade
  is purchased.

## Billing & Pricing

- Plans are billed **monthly** by default. Annual billing is available and gives
  a **20% discount** compared to paying monthly.
- Prices are quoted in **US dollars** and do not include local taxes.
- We accept major credit cards and, for Enterprise customers, invoicing.
- The Onboarding Package is a **one-time** charge, not a recurring fee.

## Refund Policy

- We offer a **30-day money-back guarantee** on the first paid month of any plan.
- Refunds are processed back to the original payment method within 5–7 business days.
- The Onboarding Package is refundable only if onboarding has not yet started.
- Annual plans can be refunded on a pro-rata basis if cancelled within the first 30 days.

## Cancellation

- Customers can cancel at any time from account settings or by contacting support.
- Cancellation takes effect at the **end of the current billing cycle** — there
  are no early-termination fees.
- After cancellation, data is retained for **30 days** in case the customer
  returns, then permanently deleted.

## Upgrades & Downgrades

- Upgrades take effect **immediately** and are prorated for the rest of the cycle.
- Downgrades take effect at the **start of the next billing cycle**.
- Moving to a smaller plan may reduce available seats and disable some features.

## Data, Privacy & Security

- Customer data is encrypted in transit and at rest.
- We **do not sell** customer data to third parties.
- Customers can request a full export or deletion of their data at any time.
- The Enterprise plan includes Single Sign-On (SSO) and audit logs.

## Support

- **Starter:** email support, responses within 2 business days.
- **Pro:** priority email support, responses within 1 business day.
- **Enterprise:** priority support plus a dedicated success manager.
- Support hours are Monday–Friday, 9am–6pm in the customer's local business hours.

## Service Level

- We target **99.9% uptime** for paid plans.
- Planned maintenance is announced at least 48 hours in advance.

## What the Agent Should NOT Do

- Do not promise discounts beyond the standard 20% annual discount.
- Do not invent features, prices, or timelines not covered here or in the
  product catalogue.
- If a question is not covered by this document, offer to have a human
  specialist follow up rather than guessing.
